M8 [pizzicati fantastiques] is written into the structure of only [A]. M8
[pizzicati fantastiques] climaxes in [A].

M16 [extremely long string tremolo descent] begins in [A] and ends at the start
of [C].

M4 [contrabass halo] is written into the structure of [A], [B], [C]. M4
[contrabass halo] territorializes the first three segments of the piece. M4
[contrabass halo] starts at the beginning of the piece. M4 [contrabass halo]
beaves as an independent layer.

M5 [extremely quiet low winds] is written into the structure of [A], [D], [E].
does M5 [extremely quiet low winds] carry an element of the sinister? not
necessarily. if the combination of contrabass clarinet and baritone saxophone
is treated as a pedal then any major or perfect interval can be used to
forestall the sinister. if the combination of contrabass clarinet and baritone
saxophone is treated as a pair of voices in counterpoint then the moments of
held conjunction between the two voices can be set to major or perfect
intervals for the same reason.

M19 [piano lightning cadenza] is written into the structure of [A], [C], [D].
M19 [piano lightning cadenza] in [A] foreshadows M19 [piano lightning cadenza]
in [C] and [D]. M19 [piano lightning cadenza] emerges from M8 [pizzicati
fantastiques] in [A]. M19 [piano lightning cadenza] remains present in [A] for
a relatively long time.

M10 [tremoli fantastiques] is written into the structure of [A] and [C]. M19
[piano lightning cadenza] and M10 [tremoli fantastiques] are bonded together in
[A] and in the rest of the piece. M19 [piano lightning cadenza] in [D] and [E]
is accompanied by M10 [tremoli fantastiques] even though M10 [tremoli
fantastiques] is not written into the structure of [D] or [E].

M6 [quiet multiphonic complexes] is written into the structure of [A] and [E].
it's not clear that the material ever climaxes. though it will be easier to get
a fair dose of the material in [A] than during the closing sequence in [E].

M5 [extremely quiet low winds] and M6 [quiet multiphonic complexes] merge into
a composite during [A].

M5 [extremely quiet low winds] and M6 [quiet multiphonic complexes] algo merge
into a composite during [E].

M8 [pizzicati fantastiques] becomes M10 [tremoli fantastiques] which in turn
becomes M12 [massed string trills].

what carries over? five layers carry over from [A] into [B]:

    (M6 [quiet multiphonic complexes])
    (M15 [woodblock underspine])
    (M16 [extremely long string tremolo descent])
    (M10 [tremoli fantastiques] ==> M12 [massed string trills])
    (M4 [contrabass halo])

SUMMARY. [A] initiates three longterm processes: M16 [extremely long string
tremolo descent], M15 [woodblock underspine] and M4 [contrabass halo]. M8
[pizzicati fantastiques] becomes M10 [tremoli fantastiques] which becomes M12
[massed string trills]. M5 [extremely quiet low winds] acts as an underlayer
and becomes M6 [quiet multiphonic complexes]. M19 [piano lightning cadenza]
appears for the first time and foreshadows M19 [piano lightning cadenza]
towards the end of the piece.
