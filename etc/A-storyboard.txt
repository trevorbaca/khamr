M8 [pizzicati fantastiques] is written into the structure of only [A]. M8
[pizzicati fantastiques] climaxes in [A].

M16 [extremely long string tremolo descent] begins in [A]. M16 [extremely long
string tremolo descent] ends at the start of [C].

M4 [contrabass halo] is written into the structure of [A], [B], [C]. M4
[contrabass halo] ends at the start of [C]. M4 [contrabass halo] beaves as an
independent layer.

M5 [extremely quiet low winds] is written into the structure of [A], [D], [E].
M5 [extremely quiet low winds] in [A] privileges major and perfect intervals to
forestall any affect of the sinister.

M19 [piano lightning cadenza] is written into the structure of [A], [C], [D].
M19 [piano lightning cadenza] in [A] foreshadows M19 [piano lightning cadenza]
in [C] and [D]. M19 [piano lightning cadenza] in [A] emerges from M8 [pizzicati
fantastiques].

M10 [tremoli fantastiques] is written into the structure of [A], [C]. M19
[piano lightning cadenza] and M10 [tremoli fantastiques] are bound together in
the piece.

M6 [quiet multiphonic complexes] is written into the structure of [A], [E]. M6
[quiet multiphonic complexes] never climaxes.

M5 [extremely quiet low winds] and M6 [quiet multiphonic complexes] merge into
a composite during [A].

M5 [extremely quiet low winds] and M6 [quiet multiphonic complexes] merge into
a composite during [E].

M8 [pizzicati fantastiques] becomes M10 [tremoli fantastiques] which in turn
becomes M12 [massed string trills].

SUMMARY. [A] initiates three longterm processes: M16 [extremely long string
tremolo descent], M15 [woodblock underspine] and M4 [contrabass halo]. M8
[pizzicati fantastiques] becomes M10 [tremoli fantastiques] which becomes M12
[massed string trills]. M5 [extremely quiet low winds] acts as an underlayer
and becomes M6 [quiet multiphonic complexes]. M19 [piano lightning cadenza]
appears for the first time and foreshadows M19 [piano lightning cadenza] later
in the piece.
