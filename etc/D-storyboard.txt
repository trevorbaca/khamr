M18 [china cymbal miasma] is written into the structure of [B] and [D]. M18
[china cymbal miasma] climaxes in [B] on the 22" cymbal. M18 [china cymbal
miasma] climaxes in [D] on the 27" cymbal.

the dynamics of M17 [snare roll] and M18 [china cymbal miasma] have to be
structured convincingly in [D].

M18 [china cymbal miasma] pairs particularly well with M12 [massed string
trills], M14 [electric string tremolo & shakers] and M20 [whirly tubes]. this
implies that M18 [china cymbal miasma] in [D] continues right up into M14
[electric string tremolo & shakers] in [D] because M14 [electric string tremolo
& shakers] is written into the structure of [D]. this means it is possible that
either M12 [massed string trills] or M20 [whirly tubes] or both may also be
present in [D].

it makes sense to include M12 [massed string trills] as a type of pervasive
element throughout [D] because M12 [massed string trills] functions as a
contrapuntal pairing to both M17 [snare roll] and M18 [china cymbal miasma].

M11 [massed off-string bowing] need not appear with M17 [snare roll] because
M12 [massed string trills] will appear with M17 [snare roll] instead.

M20 [whirly tubes] appears with M18 [china cymbal miasma] in [D].

M14 [electric string tremolo & shakers] is written into the structure of
segments [A], [C], [D] and [E]. M14 [electric string tremolo & shakers]
climaxes only in [D] and [E]. M14 [electric string tremolo & shakers] functions
as a type of stripped-down foreshadowing in [A] and [C].

the full scoring of M14 [electric string tremolo & shakers] features fff
piccolo fluttertongue and struck tam-tam. both materials occur [D] and [E].

M20 [whirly tubes] constitutes a strong pairing with M14 [electric string
tremolo & shakers].

M19 [piano lightning cadenza] is written into the structure of segments [A],
[C] and [D]. M14 [electric string tremolo & shakers] and M19 [piano lightning
cadenza] abut in [D] and work in counterpoint. 

[D-E] houses the climax of M14 [electric string tremolo & shakers].

[D-E] also houses the climax of M19 [piano lightning cadenza].

M10 [tremoli fantastiques] follows M19 [piano lightning cadenza] in [A] and
[C]. M10 [tremoli fantastiques] is bonded to M19 [piano lightning cadenza] in
the piece.

M19 [piano lightning cadenza] is accompanied by M10 [tremoli fantastiques] in
[D] even though M10 [tremoli fantastiques] is not written into the structure of
[D].

the true climax of M19 [piano lightning cadenza] comes in [E]. the true climax
of M19 [piano lightning cadenza] in [E] is accompanied by a saturated version
of M7 [ferocious multiphonic complexes] even though M7 [ferocrious multiphonic
complexes] is written into only the end of [B].
