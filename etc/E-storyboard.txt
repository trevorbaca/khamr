ensemble both before and after the appearance of the fff rattan. percussionist
returns to M18 [china cymbal miasma] afterwards.

bowing] is a type of foam used to highlight M19 [piano lightning cadenza].

fff shakers. M14 [electric string tremolo and shakers] subjects string tremolo
to decrescendo and shakers to increasing density during [E]. M14 [electric
string tremolo and shakers] combines extremely well with M1 [upper-register
prestissimo].

orchestrated in [C]. M2A [seaweed guiro] is orchestrated in a dreamlike way in
[E].
